HOW TO PLAY

Tic Tac Toe is a simple and fun game for two players.

The goal is to get three of your marks (either "X" or "O") in a row, either

horizontally, vertically, or diagonally, before your opponent does.

INSTRUCTION

Player 1 starts by placing an "X" in one of the empty boxes.

Player 2 then places an "O" in one of the remaining empty boxes.

Players take turns marking a square until one of the following happens:

A player wins by placing three of their marks in a row,

either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

The game ends in a draw if all boxes are filled and no player has won.

After a game is over, click the Play Again button to start a new game.
